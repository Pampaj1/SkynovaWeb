The Drone Service Station is a robust system designed for efficient drone operation management. It includes:

1. Flight Management: Operators assign drones to calls, which then receive coordinates, execute tasks, and return to the hangar.
2. Power Supply: The station uses the main power grid and has emergency batteries powered by solar panels, providing 6-8 hours of backup.
3. Safety: Electrical safety features include surge protection and fire prevention mechanisms.
4. Central Computer: Assists in drone assignment and tasks, with options for fiber optic connectivity.
5. Gates: Aluminum roller gates powered by electric motors, integrated with the central computer.
6. Charging Modules: Uses QI wireless charging stations.
7. Space Organization: Data and power are managed via Ethernet cables.
8. Security: Equipped with cameras, motion detection, and biometric authentication using Aldahua ASI6214S systems.

This system ensures efficient, safe, and reliable management of drone operations.
